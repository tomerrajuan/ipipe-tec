Welcome to ipipe

The Challenge

Water distribution networks - which are mostly underground - are the world's largest engineering infrastructures (hundreds of millions of km) that do not receive a direct periodic inspection.

Partial or no solutions exist to the wide spectrum of challenges facing water network operators: leaks, faulty welds, corrosion, blockage, pirate connections and unmapped (GIS) network segments.

The Solution

iPIPE's innovative solution is a mini wireless device ("submarine") capable of maneuvering inside active pressurized water distribution networks while they are in use, inspecting and providing precise information regarding pipe and network conditions, system integrity, and location.

<4> in the video: MODA_01 is traveling inside a pipe in our Laboratory.

The Technology

MODA_01 is an autonomous sensor carrying platform that will travel substantial distances, navigate independently and is capable of communicating with an operator.

The core capabilities are:

1. Navigation - device locating and response to obstacles in pressurized water networks.
2. Inspection - implement visual, noise, pressure, temp, flow and other sensing devices.
3. Communication - signals transfer in water pipes.

About Us

"iPIPE Ltd." is a start-up company managin a revolutionary system that provides direct inspection and asset management capability of potable water pressurized pipe networks while the pipe is still active.

We provide effective solutions for identifying and locating leaks, faulty welds, corrosion, blockages, unauthorized connections, and unmapped network segments.
